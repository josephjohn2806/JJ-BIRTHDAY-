Our Story

Where the Wing Lands and Adventure Rolls

Musembi Earthways Safaris was born from a desire to reframe traditional African travel. We bridge luxury, wild tracking, and community-led conservancy design into seamless journeys.

We work directly alongside local tracking specialists to ensure every tour preserves wildlife habitats while providing a completely custom guest experience.

"Honoring raw Kenyan nature, supporting local heritages."
